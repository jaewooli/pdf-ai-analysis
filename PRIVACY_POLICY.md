# Privacy Policy for PDF AI Analyzer Pro

"PDF AI Analyzer Pro" (the "Extension") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. Information Collection and Processing

The Extension processes the following types of information:

- **PDF Document Content**: When you initiate an analysis, the Extension extracts text or the PDF file itself (in Base64 format) from the currently open document.
- **AI API Keys**: The Extension stores API keys (e.g., OpenAI, Google Gemini, DeepSeek) that you provide in the settings.
- **Analysis History**: The Extension saves the results of your past analyses (summaries, etc.).

## 2. Use of Information

The information is used solely for the following purposes:

- **Providing AI Analysis**: Extracted content is sent to your selected AI provider (Ollama, OpenAI, Gemini, or DeepSeek) to generate summaries and insights.
- **Maintaining Settings and History**: API keys and past results are stored locally for your convenience across sessions.

## 3. Third-Party Data Sharing

The Extension shares PDF data with the AI service provider you select.

- **Recipients**: OpenAI, Google, DeepSeek, or your local environment (e.g., Ollama).
- **Data Shared**: Extracted text or PDF file data.
- **Important Note**: Please refer to the privacy policies of the respective AI providers for information on how they handle your data.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage Only**: The Extension does **not** store your data on any central server. All data, including API keys and analysis history, is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
- **Security**: Your API keys are never sent to our servers; they are used only for direct communication with the AI providers.

## 5. User Controls and Rights

You have full control over your data:

- **Clear History**: You can delete all stored analysis history via the "Clear History" button in the Extension's side panel.
- **Manage Settings**: You can update or remove your API keys at any time through the settings panel.
- **Uninstallation**: Deleting the Extension from your browser will also remove all locally stored data associated with it.

## 6. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
